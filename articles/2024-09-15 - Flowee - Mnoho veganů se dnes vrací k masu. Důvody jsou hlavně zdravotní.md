## Sumarizace relevantnosti

Článek se zabývá tématem návratu bývalých veganů k konzumaci masa, což z něj činí veganský rámec dominantním - rozvíjí konkrétní případy (Irvin St-Louis, Alexandra Ashbacková) a diskutuje zdravotní důvody pro opuštění veganism. Environmentální rámec je přítomen marginálně - článek zmíňuje výzkumy o zdravotních přínosech rostlinné stravy, ale bez rozvíjené argumentace o environmentálním dopadě masové konzumace nebo sustainability.

## Text článku

Choreographer Irvin St-Louis, vegan since 1996, experienced joint inflammation, tendon inflammation, and back pain after nearly two decades without animal products. He eventually incorporated meat into his diet after discovering meat-focused content on social media.

Content creator Alexandra Ashbacková followed a plant-based diet for three years but developed severe fatigue, migraines, and nutritional deficiencies including critically low iron, B12, vitamin A, D, and zinc levels. After reintroducing fish and other animal products, she reported improved sleep, energy, skin clarity, and reduced menstrual symptoms.

The article notes that celebrities including Miley Cyrus, Zac Efron, Arnold Schwarzenegger, and Mike Tyson have publicly abandoned veganism, citing issues like cognitive dysfunction and digestive problems.

However, registered dietitian Andrea Rymer maintains that "plant-based diets including fortified foods and supplements can provide all necessary nutrients." The article acknowledges research showing veganism's cardiovascular benefits while highlighting nutritional gaps including deficiencies in B vitamins, iodine, zinc, calcium, and selenium. Experts recommend consulting dietitians before adopting restrictive dietary plans.

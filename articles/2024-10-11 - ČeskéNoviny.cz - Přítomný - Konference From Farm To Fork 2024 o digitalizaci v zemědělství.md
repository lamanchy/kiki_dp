# From Farm To Fork 2024 Conference

The article announces the **From Farm TO Fork 2024 conference** scheduled for November 13, 2024, at the Clarion Congress Hotel in Prague's Vysočany district.

## Key Topics

The all-day event focuses on three main areas:
- New trends in Czech food supply
- Food waste prevention
- Agricultural and food industry digitalization

## Featured Speakers and Discussions

The conference includes presentations on:

- **Food retail trends**: Tomáš Prouza will discuss "five hottest trends in food supply" and how "customer requirements for quality changed after inflation subsided"

- **Food safety inspections**: Michaela Mezerová covers State Agricultural and Food Inspection findings, including ongoing issues with food quality, hygiene, and double-standard products

- **IFS audit compliance**: DQS Slovakia representatives address common non-conformities found during audits, focusing on documentation, hygiene, and allergen management

- **Quality standards**: GLOBALG.A.P. certification supports sustainable agricultural production with safe working conditions

- **Food waste reduction**: Jaroslav Koláček from PENNY MARKET explains discount retailers' anti-waste strategies, including partnerships with food banks

- **Agricultural digitalization**: Matěj Sklenář demonstrates practical technology applications reducing waste and improving production quality

The conference targets agricultural companies, retail chains, and food safety professionals. More information is available at www.farmfork.csq.cz.
